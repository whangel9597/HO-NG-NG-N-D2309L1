STORY

Jujutsu Kaisen (呪術廻戦じゅしゅつかいせん Jujutsu kaisen?) is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Gege Akutami, published in Shueisha's Weekly Shōnen Jump magazine since March 2018. The series was released by Shueisha and has 27 volumes published as of July 2024. The story follows Itadori Yuji, a high school boy who secretly joins the Jujutsu Sorcerer organization with the ambition to destroy a powerful cursed spirit named Ryoumen Sukuna.

AUTHOR-GEGE AKUTAMI

Gege Akutami (芥あくた見み下げ々げ Akutami Gege) is a Japanese manga artist and the author of Jujutsu Kaisen.
In 2018, Gege Akutami started their first full serialization with Jujutsu Kaisen in issue 14 of Weekly Shonen Jump. Jujutsu Kaisen serves as the follow-up series to Jujutsu Kaisen 0: Jujutsu High.

CHARACTERS

ITADORI YUJI

Yuji Itadori (虎いた杖どり悠ゆう仁じ Itadori Yūji) is the protagonist of the Jujutsu Kaisen series. He is the son of Jin Itadori and Kaori Itadori, and the grandson of Wasuke Itadori. Yuji was living a normal life in Sendai City until he met Megumi and ate one of Sukuna's fingers. After becoming Sukuna's ship, Yuji began attending Tokyo Jujutsu College along with Megumi and Nobara as freshmen.

MEGUMI FUSHIGURO

Megumi Fushiguro (伏ふし黒ぐろ恵めぐみ Fushiguro Megumi) is the deuteragonist of the Jujutsu Kaisen series. He is a second-level jujutsu sorcerer and a freshman at Tokyo Jujutsu College along with Yuji Itadori and Nobara Kugisaki. Megumi is the stepbrother of Tsumiki Fushiguro and the son of Toji Fushiguro. Megumi was born outside the clan as Toji's trump card and was eventually found by Gojo.

NOBARA KUGISAKI

Nobara Kugisaki (釘くぎ崎さき野の薔ば薇ら Kugisaki Nobara) is the third character of the Jujutsu Kaisen series. She's a freshman, a third-year jujutsu sorcerer at Tokyo Jujutsu College, studying under Gojo along with Yuji and Megumi. Nobara is a confident,bold young woman with an unshakable personality. More than anything, Nobara was determined to be true to himself no matter what happened.

SATORU GOJO

Satoru Gojo (五ご条じょう悟さとる Gojō Satoru) is one of the main characters of the Jujutsu Kaisen series. He is a special-level jujutsu sorcerer and is widely recognized as the strongest person in the world. Satoru is the pride of the Gojo Family, the first to inherit both Infinity and Six Eyes in four hundred years. He works as a teacher at Tokyo Jujutsu College and uses his influence to protect and train powerful young allies.

MAKI ZENIN

Maki Zenin (禪ぜん院いん真ま希き Zen Maki) is a main supporting character in Jujutsu Kaisen and one of the main characters of its, Jujutsu Kaisen 0: Jujutsu High. She is currently in her second year at Tokyo Jujutsu College. The daughter of Ogi Zenin and Zenin Mai's twin sister, Maki was born into the Zenin Clan. Born not a witch in one of the three great wizarding families, Maki is constantly mistreated & feels compelled to expel herself from the Zenin family.

TOGE INUMAKI

Toge Inumaki (狗いぬ巻まき棘とげ Inumaki Toge) is a minor character in Jujutsu Kaisen and one of the main characters of its prequel, Jujutsu Kaisen 0: Jujutsu High. He is a descendant of the Inumaki clan and has inherited the clan's curse, for which he has developed his own unique speech pattern to avoid cursing others. Toge is currently a semi-level 1st and second year jujutsu wizard at Tokyo Jujutsu College along with classmates Yuta, Maki, and Panda.

PANDA

Panda (パンダ Panda) is a major supporting character in Jujutsu Kaisen and one of the main characters, Jujutsu Kaisen 0: Jujutsu High. Despite his name, Panda is not a real panda, he is a Cursed Corpse suddenly created by Masamichi Yaga. He attended Magic College as a second year student along with Maki, Toge and Yuta. After a fight with Hajime Kashimo that resulted in the destruction of his body and two other cores, Panda now appears much smaller than his original size.

YUTA OKKOTSU

Yuta Okkotsu (乙おっ骨こつ憂ゆう太た Okkotsu Yūta) is a main supporting character in the Jujutsu Kaisen series, Jujutsu Kaisen 0: Jujutsu High. He was originally a special level cursed human haunted by his late childhood friend, Orimoto Rika. Satoru Gojo mentored Yuta and enrolled him in Tokyo Magic College. Yuta is currently a second year student at Spell College, where he works as a spell master. He trained abroad in Africa with Miguel until after the Shibuya incident when he returned to Tokyo.

SUKUNA

Ryomen Sukuna (両りょう面めん宿すく儺な Ryōmen Sukuna) was the strongest spellcaster from over a thousand years ago. Sukuna is one of the main antagonists of the Jujutsu Kaisen series. Sukuna exists through a state where he divides his power into twenty indestructible fingers. Sukuna incarnated in June 2018 as Yuji Itadori consumed his fingers. Sukuna was trapped inside Yuji's body and barely in control until he transferred his soul into Megumi's body.

SUGURU GETOU

Suguru Geto (夏げ油とう傑すぐる Getō Suguru) is an antagonist in both the Jujutsu Kaisen series and the prequel Jujutsu Kaisen 0: Jujutsu High. He was originally a student of Masamichi Yaga along with Satoru Gojo and Shoko Ieiri at Tokyo Jujutsu High. His experiences as a necromancer fostered a deep hatred for non-wizards, leading to an incident where Suguru massacred over a hundred civilians in one night.

KENTO NANAMI

Kento Nanami (七なな海み建けん人と Nanami Kento) is a main supporting character in Jujutsu Kaisen. He is a former student of Tokyo Magic College, where he was a junior student of Satoru Gojo and Suguru Geto. Nanami initially left the Spell College after graduating to become a salaryman, but returned four years later to continue working as a spellcaster. With Satoru's introduction, he also became a close mentor to Yuji.

MAHITO

Mahito (真ま人ひと Mahito) is one of the primary antagonists of the Jujutsu Kaisen series. He is an unregistered special grade cursed spirit allied with Pseudo-Geto. He is also the leader of his own group, consisting of Jogo, Hanami, and Dagon. His group's ultimate goal is the eradication of humanity and ultimately replacing the population with cursed spirits.
